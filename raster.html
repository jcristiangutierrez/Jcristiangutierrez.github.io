Raster Graphics

I first got introduced to graphic design back in 2018, I had previously forgotten my roots as an artists prior to being introduced due to trying to chase a dream that did not fully connect to me like creating art does. Once I came into contact with these new art medium, to be more specific than just use the broad term of graphic design. The main software used for the creation of these various graphics was Adobe Photoshop. Raster graphics different than vector are basically graphics that rely on imagery and pixels instead points and paths like vectors. I made all these art piece for my clothing brand VAV (violenceagainstviolence) meaning various things the best explanation I can give in order for you to understand my vision is comparing it to fighting fire against fire. I shall let your minds wander with that. But I have had this company since 2018 and in these past few years I have been practicng my art and creating these visuals so I can put them on clothing. A main reason for this either than the money aspect of it is the ability to share my art with the world and express myself like no other and show everyone the thoughts that run through my head all day. I used various tools to create this various effects I can go on and on but I honestly have reached a point where creating graphics is second nature to me, where I do not think I just do. Currently this year I am really pushing to market my company more. I have no specific dates for the creations of these pieces, but the timeframe ranges from 2019 to present time.
