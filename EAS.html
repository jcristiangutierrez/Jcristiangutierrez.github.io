Emergency Alert System

For my final porject in Art 74, I created a propaganda video to illustrate the corrupt world we live in. In today's world the top elites run everything, they are heavily influential on the stock market, trade, capitalism, and etc. We the people who are not fortunate enough to endulge in the rich lavish life of the elites. Must suffer the consequences of their created Capitalistic system meant to prey on the weak so that the rich may thrive. The main reason I was inspired to create this video was actually due to an Anthropology class I took last semseter which focused on global culture and capitalism. I already had a sense of the extortion core countries put forth onto periphery countries but it is worst than I thought. It always is. Corporations partaking with places that still enslave children for labor and even abducting them from their homes or all the workers across the world getting a piece rate lower than the what the commodity the made is being sold for. All around the world people are being kept in poverty due to capitalism and it's ways. The mediums used to create this video were After Effects and Audacity. I used After Effects in order to create the animation of the globe and texts, as well as using after effects as my main area of editing the stock videos as well which I found free online. To generate the speech throughout the video, I used a text to speech application where I just proceed to type what I wanted to say, then recording the audio onto audacity. Once in audacity I altered the voice in order to create a "JigSaw" voice. Created NOV 2020
