The Pythoness Lair

The process I took took in creating this piece involved my skills with modeling in Blender as well as incorporating my skills in photogrammetry. The following cliff side as well as the haunting face were photogrammetry scans that I gathered for use in this project. The following entrance and door way were modeled and texture within Blender. When viewing the piece what I want the viewer to see and feel as if they are playing their favorite RPG game and they are standing outside of the Bosses lair. My main inspiration when creating this piece was a game that recently came out and that is Tiny Tina's Wonderland, a side story within the Borderlands series. March 2022
